Mari 2

Maari 2 is a 2018 Indian Tamil-language action-comedy film directed by Balaji Mohan and produced by Dhanush under his production banner Wunderbar Films. It is a sequel to the 2015 film Maari and stars Dhanush, Sai Pallavi, Tovino Thomas, Varalaxmi Sarathkumar, and Krishna Kulasekaran in lead roles. The film follows the adventures of Maari, a local gangster who rules his neighborhood with an iron fist. Maari is a flamboyant and charismatic character who is loved by his followers and feared by his enemies. However, his life takes an unexpected turn when a new gangster, Beeja, enters the scene and challenges his authority. The film features high-octane action sequences, witty one-liners, and a strong soundtrack that complements the film's fast-paced narrative. The chemistry between Dhanush and Sai Pallavi, who plays the role of an auto driver, adds an extra layer of charm to the film. Maari 2 was well-received by audiences and critics alike, who praised its energetic performances, stylish visuals, and entertaining storyline. The film's music, composed by Yuvan Shankar Raja, was particularly popular, with several songs becoming chartbusters. Overall, Maari 2 is a fun-filled and entertaining film that is sure to appeal to fans of action-comedies. It showcases Dhanush's versatility as an actor and cements his status as one of the leading stars of Tamil cinema. The film's success has also paved the way for a possible third installment in the franchise, which is eagerly awaited by fans
