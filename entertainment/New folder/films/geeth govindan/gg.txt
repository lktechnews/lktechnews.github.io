"Geetha Govindam" is a 2018 Indian Telugu-language romantic comedy film directed by Parasuram and produced by Bunny Vasu under the banner of GA2 Pictures. The film stars Vijay Deverakonda and Rashmika Mandanna in the lead roles.

The story follows Vijay Govind (Vijay Deverakonda), a young college professor who falls in love with Geetha (Rashmika Mandanna), a strong-willed woman he meets on a bus journey. However, Vijay's attempts to impress Geetha are misunderstood, and she begins to hate him. Things get more complicated when Geetha's brother Phaneendra (Subbaraju) mistakes Vijay for his sister's lover and starts attacking him.

As the story unfolds, Vijay tries to win Geetha's heart and prove his innocence to her brother. The film's comedy elements are woven into the romantic storyline, with the film's many humorous scenes providing plenty of laughs for audiences.

The film's soundtrack, composed by Gopi Sundar, features popular songs such as "Inkem Inkem Inkem Kaavaale", which became a massive hit among Telugu music lovers. The film's cinematography by Manikandan also received praise for its vibrant and colorful visuals.

"Geetha Govindam" was a commercial success, grossing over 130 crore rupees worldwide. The film was also well-received critically, with particular praise given to Vijay Deverakonda and Rashmika Mandanna's performances. The chemistry between the two leads was also highly praised, with many citing it as one of the film's main strengths.

Overall, "Geetha Govindam" is a fun and entertaining romantic comedy that showcases the talents of its lead actors and crew. The film's catchy music and vibrant visuals add to its overall appeal, making it a must-watch for fans of Telugu cinema.
